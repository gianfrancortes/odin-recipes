Lasagna

Description

This is a time-consuming but delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
